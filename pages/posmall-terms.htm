Terms

Starter terms for the POSMall storefront.

Products, services, and digital items

POSMall may list physical products, digital items, and posmallServices. Product information, prices, availability, and service details may change before an order is accepted.

Orders and payment

Orders must be submitted through POSMall checkout. Payment may be processed by enabled providers such as Stripe or PayPal. Do not use real payment details in test mode.

Shipping and taxes

Shipping options, delivery estimates, and taxes depend on cart contents, destination, and active store configuration. Some products or services may not require shipping.

Returns and refunds

Returns and refunds placeholder: add the store operator policy before production, including timing, eligibility, digital-item limits, service cancellation rules, and contact instructions.

Accounts

Customers are responsible for keeping account access secure and for providing accurate order, billing, and shipping information.

Acceptable use

Do not misuse checkout, account, payment, review, support, or Favorites features. Fraud, abuse, or attempts to disrupt the storefront may result in order cancellation or account restriction.
