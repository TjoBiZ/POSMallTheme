Privacy Policy

Starter privacy and data notice for the POSMall storefront.

Data we collect

We collect the information needed to run account, cart, checkout, order, shipping, digital-product, service-product, and support workflows. This can include name, email, phone, billing address, shipping address, order contents, account preferences, support messages, and technical session data.

Payments

Payment details are handled by enabled payment processors such as Stripe and PayPal. POSMall should not ask you to send card numbers, passwords, or payment credentials by email or support message.

Cookies and session storage

We use essential cookies or browser storage for login sessions, cart state, checkout progress, Favorites, security, and cookie notice preferences.

How data is used

Data is used to create accounts, process orders, collect payment through providers, calculate shipping and taxes, deliver physical or digital products, schedule services, prevent abuse, and respond to support requests.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of eligible personal information. Some order, tax, fraud-prevention, payment, or legal records may need to be retained.
